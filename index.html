Tousie: is a board game, which players play to get in the inner place or the throne first using four coins as their lucky number for moving forward to reach to the inner throne. Furthermore, the four coins have five probabilities, which is 1: "Head Head Head Head" makes the player to move eight step forward, 2:"Head Head Head Tail" makes the player to return back one step it means Toussi, 3:"Head Head Tail Tail" makes the player to move forward two step, 4:"Head Tail Tail Tail" makes the player to move forward three step, and 5: "Tail Tail Tail Tail" makes the player to move four step forward. Beside this the game has rules 1: if players pass or just get other players position, the one who were standing at the position will return back to zero or his Home. However if the players were staying at his home or other players home, it has a privillage to not be killed, and it will stay as it is. 2: The one who reaches the throne first is the winner, and it will continue untill one player left, so this one will be the looser. 3: When players get four("Tail Tail Tail Tail") or eight("Head Head Head Head"), they will get extra life, one extra life for the former and two extra life for the later, which makes them to try their chance again apart from their turn.
